Nature

Nature is beautiful. The leaves are colorful as they change from green to orange every fall. The trees are so tall. Trees are my world because they let me breathe. That fresh air smells like a breeze. Then there are flowers with their scent. One smells like jasmine and the next is a rose. The colors are so bright. That is why I love nature because it is my happy place.

Discussion Questions

  • What season do the leaves change from green to orange?

  • Name two flower scents in the story.

  • What do trees let us do?

The Tooth Fairy

Yesterday I lost my first tooth. It hurt a lot but I knew it would be worth it. I fell asleep that day hopeful that the tooth fairy would visit me. I tucked my little tooth under my pillow. I had a dream that night that the tooth fairy sprinkled some pixie dust along with some coins and fluttered away. The very next day I jumped out of bed to see a big bag of chocolate coins.

Discussion Questions

  • What two things did the tooth fairy sprinkle?

  • What type of coins were there the next morning?

  • Is this the first or second tooth that was lost?

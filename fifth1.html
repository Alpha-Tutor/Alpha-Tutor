The Food Diagram

When we eat, some foods are good for us. We need food like milk, bread, and greens. These foods help us grow and give us good minerals. But it does not always have to be bread, it can also be foods like rice, wheat, or pasta. And for milk, it can be cheese, meat, eggs, or beans. Greens can be vegetables or fruits.

Words

Words can be hurtful but nice if you say good things. If you are nice, your friends will be nice to you too. But if you say bad things, then your friends will not feel good. Every person can feel good and that is very nice. You can say your words with your pencil, a song, or your mouth. Be smart with your words.

Discussion Questions

  • Fill in the blanks: Be ___ with your words.

  • How can you say your words?

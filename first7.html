Princess Dream

Once upon a time, there was a young girl who dreamed she was a princess. Every night she slept dreaming about a shiny tiara on her head. One night as she slept, a tiara floated down slowly on her head. She was in a big, poofy princess dress too! She waved to her kingdom. She never wanted to leave her castle. It was everything she ever wanted.

Discussion Questions

  • What did the young girl dream to be?

  • How often did she dream about a tiara?

  • Fill in the blanks: The princess dress was ___ and ___.

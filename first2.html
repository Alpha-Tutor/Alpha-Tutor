Our Face

As you know, we have eyes, a nose, a mouth, and more face parts. Our eyes let us see and our nose lets us breathe and smell. Our mouth lets us eat and drink food. Being able to taste, hear, see, and speak, and feel are our senses. We also have eyebrows to stop dust and dirt from getting into our eyes. Our eyelashes do this too. Humans have many cool parts to help us live our lives.

Discussion Questions

  • What two face parts have similar jobs?

  • Which part of the face lets us eat?

  • What are the five senses?

Answers

  • Mouth.

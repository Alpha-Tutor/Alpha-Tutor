Shooting Star

Today was the day I was going to see a shooting star for the first time! The news reported there was going to be a shooting star shower where there would be thousands of shooting starts happening at once right out in my backyard! My older sister Magdelyne and my parents had set up a picnic in the backyard with our neighbors so we could enjoy it while eating my favorite foods too. We sat down and waited for a few minutes until finally, I saw the first one. It flew through the sky like a jet going at a billion meters per second. It was so quick like a flash, like someone just shot me with a needle. I closed my eyes and made a wish for a hundred more wishes. Magdalene said I can’t do that, but I did and I was right. Seconds later a hundred more shooting stars came. And I wished on every single one.

Art

Art is an expression,
A statement or a question,
Art is a passion,
For drawing or fashion.

Art is a story,
To be told is a glory,
A poem or a book,
With meaning or just a look.

Art is infinite,
It is imperfect, doesn’t have to fit,
Art is nature, everywhere,
It is as rare as air.

Art is a person,
To better or to worsen,
Art is abstract or real,
It depends on the person to appeal.

Art can be made by anyone,
It can be lost or it can be done,
Whether it’s ugly or pretty,
Or it’s left in the dirt to pity.

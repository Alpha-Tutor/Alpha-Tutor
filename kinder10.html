Rainbows

When the sky is blue,
So pretty and bright,
I am so happy too,
All around, there is light.

There was just rain,
It smells like earth,
There is now a stain,
There is no more hurt.

A rainbow is here,
From the rain and the Sun,
The sky is so clear,
In the rainbow, I will run.

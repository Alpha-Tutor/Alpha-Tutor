Kitty Cat

Kitty Cats can purr and they say “meow” to us. They walk on four legs and are very cool. They are house pets but some live on the streets in some places. Some are not loud and some are. There are many colors and shapes that they have. Kitty Cats are very cute but be careful when you play with them.

Alpha Tutor©

At Alpha Tutor, we are dedicated to making high-quality tutoring resources available to all tutors, parents, and teachers at no cost. Our goal is to support young learners in Pre-K through 5th grade by providing engaging stories, reading materials, and discussion questions to help them grow in English and reading comprehension.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the chance to become a confident reader and communicator. Alpha Tutor was created to help students build strong language skills through fun and interactive stories, poems, and non-fiction texts. Our resources are designed to help students thrive in a safe and nurturing learning environment.

Everything We Offer is Free!

We want to ensure that all tutors and parents have the resources they need, which is why we’re offering our materials completely free of charge. The only thing we ask is that you share Alpha Tutor with others who might benefit from it! The more we spread the word, the more young learners we can help.

How Can You Help?

If you find our resources helpful, we’d love for you to share Alpha Tutor with your friends, family, and colleagues. Whether you're a tutor, teacher, or parent, helping others learn about us can make a big difference in the lives of students who need these resources the most.

Featured Resources

We offer 10 stories per grade level (4 non-fiction, 4 fiction, and 2 poems) along with discussion questions to help guide students through each piece. Our resources are designed to be fun, educational, and meaningful for young learners.

Explore Our Grade-Specific Resources:

  • Kindergarten: Fun, interactive stories that introduce early literacy concepts.

  • First Grade: Stories to help build vocabulary and develop reading comprehension skills.

  • Second Grade: Creative stories and factual texts to expand young minds.

  • Third Grade: Engaging narratives and non-fiction pieces to challenge young readers.

  • Fourth Grade: Fiction and non-fiction stories that encourage critical thinking.

  • Fifth Grade: Challenging texts designed to strengthen reading and comprehension skills.

Why Alpha Tutor?

Alpha Tutor is a place where learning is both fun and effective. Our goal is to give tutors and parents the tools they need to guide children toward a bright future. Best of all, our resources are completely free—no subscription required, no payment needed, just learning at its best.

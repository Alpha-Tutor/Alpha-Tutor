Santa Comes to Town

I could barely sleep the night before Christmas. Emmy was snoring next to me but I had tried my best to stay awake just in case Santa came up to my room! I gave him a long list- but not too long- I asked for a pony, some new headbands, and a new bicycle. Mom said that was way too much to ask, but even if I get one, that would be okay. I jumped out of bed and shook Emmy awake, she was such a sleepyhead. We ran downstairs to see the Christmas tree surrounded by four gifts. I had given one to Emmy and she gave one to me. And our parents had given both of us one. I opened my gifts to find some new headbands from my parents and a toy pony from my sister Emmy. This was the best Christmas ever!

Discussion Questions

  • What three things did the writer ask for and what did she get?

  • Why is it important not to ask too much for Christmas?

  • How many gifts did Emmy receive and from who?

Answers

  • She asked for a pony, some new headbands, and a new bicycle, she got some new headbands and a toy pony .

  • It’s unlikely to get everything you ask for so it’s important to separate your needs and wants .

  • She received two, one from her parents and one from her sister, Emmy.

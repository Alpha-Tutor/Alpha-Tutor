Constellations

Orion is a hunter,
With three stars in a line,
Leo is a lion,
In the shape of a backwards question mark.

Aries is a ram,
With stars shaped in a “V”
Scorpius is a scorpion,
The brightest in the sky.

Aquarius is a water bearer,
Shaped like a jar,
Pegasus is a horse,
With the shape of its frame.

Ursa Minor, Ursa Major,
Little Dipper, Big Dipper,,
Two bears crawling around,
In the sky, lost and found.

Corona Borealis is a crown,
Called the Blaze Star,
Shown in spring and summer,
Shaped like an upside down arc.

Constellations are found in the sky,
Made up of stars that appear smaller than your eye,
Though some are bigger than the Sun, they are too far, too high,
In different patterns, they lie.

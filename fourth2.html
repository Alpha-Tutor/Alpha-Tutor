The New Seven Wonders

The New Seven Wonders of the World are incredible places that people around the world voted for in 2007. These special places are:

1. The Great Wall of China is a long wall built a long time ago to keep enemies away.

2. Petra in Jordan is an ancient city carved right into the rock cliffs.

3. Christ the Redeemer in Brazil is a giant statue of Jesus that watches over the city of Rio de Janeiro.

4. Machu Picchu in Peru is an old city high up in the mountains, made by the Inca people a long time ago.

5. Chichen Itza in Mexico is an old Mayan city with a big pyramid.

6. The Roman Colosseum in Italy is a huge stadium where gladiators once fought.

7. The Taj Mahal in India is a beautiful white marble tomb made by a king for his wife.

These wonders are famous for their beauty and history. People from all over the world visit these places to see how amazing they are and to learn about the people who made them. Each wonder shows how clever and hardworking people can be when they create things that last a long time.

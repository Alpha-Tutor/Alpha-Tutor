Feelings

Feelings are okay,
Be nice to you and me,
When I play,
I feel happy.

When I feel sad,
I can cry,
And when I feel mad,
I will still try.

All feelings are good,
But being happy is the best,
You should feel as you should,
And let yourself rest.

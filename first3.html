The Sun

We have a Sun that we see in the sky. It is the center of our universe and the Earth goes around the Sun. It gives us heat and light for plants, food, and we need it for everything. It looks yellow but it is white. It is one of the many different stars in the universe. It is so hot, if Earth was even a tiny bit closer to the Sun, we would burn up. Without the Sun, we would not live and nothing ever could.

Animals

There are thousands and thousands of different animals on Earth including humans. Different types of animals can be separated by their habitat, bone structure, and survival traits. For example, there are herbivores, omnivores, and carnivores. Herbivores only eat plants like giraffes, omnivores eat both plants and other animals like bears, and carnivores only eat other animals like sharks.

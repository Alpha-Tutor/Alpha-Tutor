Fish

Fish live in water. They can swim. They can be as small as rocks or as big as you! They can see and get air in the water. They do not have hands or legs but they have fins to swim. They are very fast too. They swim in packs called schools. They eat food that lives in the water. There are a lot of fish in the world.
